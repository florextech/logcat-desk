# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and constructive collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Give technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about problems and tradeoffs.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the software and community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community interactions connected to the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or close content that violates these expectations, and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately instead of escalating publicly in issue threads.
